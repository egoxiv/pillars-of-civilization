Pillars of Civilization

    How to play

  • - PoC is a two player game, both players will start by entering their names.
  • - After both player have entered their names, click next turn to get all of your money and military units
  • - By default, Player 1 is America and Player 2 is Britain
  • - Every 3 turns, you get to make a decision that will impact your gameplay.
  • - The decisions are either military or economy focused. There are no wrong decisions, you simply want to make the best one based on your strategy and your opponents.
  • - Keep an eye on your military and gold because you will need as much as possible to win the game at the end of 15 turns. The more military units you have, the more damage you will do to your opponent.
  • - Be careful with bills, they will be subtract from your final total plus an additional 15% if they are unpaid at the end of 15 turns.

How to win

Your total gold, military units, and total damage of your combined military units will be added up and the player with the higher total wins.
